About the Server

GamesOnFire is a thriving Minecraft network packed with exciting game modes, a friendly community, and non-stop action. Jump in and feel the heat!

⚔️
PvP Practice

Intense PvP battles with ranked matchmaking, custom kits, and seasonal tournaments. Prove you're the best!

🥊
BoxPvP

Fight to the top in intense 1v1 and free-for-all box arenas. Master your combos and become the ultimate champion!

🏛️
Pillars of Fortune

Race up towering pillars, defeat your opponents, and claim the fortune at the peak. One wrong step and it's over!

🌍
Survival SMP

A lively survival world with custom enchantments, player shops, quests, and monthly world events.

💎
Custom Ranks

Earn or purchase exclusive ranks with unique perks, cosmetics, and in-game advantages.

🔒
Anti-Cheat

Advanced anti-cheat systems and an active moderation team keep gameplay fair and fun for everyone.
